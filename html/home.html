What is multimedia...

Multimedia is the convergence of text, pictures, video and sound into a single form. Creative industries use multimedia for a variety of purposes, from fine arts to entertainment, commercial art, journalism, etc. Multimedia artists combine several techniques using different media that in some way, the viewer can interact with. Multimedia implies tact in both production and presentation, is more than simple images and text.

The multimedia technician...

The Multimedia Technician is a qualified professional able to perform jobs related to the design and production of digital linear content (such as movies) or non-linear contents (interactive navigation) . Performs both technical and artistic tasks.

Core competencies:

  • Create 2D / 3D images and animations for multimedia applications.
  • Design / develop interactive multimedia products.
  • Designing multimedia contents.
  • Edit content to create both informative or entertaining communication solutions.
  • Capture , scan and treat images, sounds and text.
  • Content integration using authoring tools.
  • Programming multimedia applications.
